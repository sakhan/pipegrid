Leverage Social Media!

PipeGrid Makes it Easy to Share Your Inventory

6 Tips for Selling with Social Media

In addition to offering the most powerful online Pipe & Tubing database, PipeGrid is the first site designed to help you leverage the power of social media in order to move inventory.

Think social media is just for teens and young people? Think again, LinkedIn has over 135 million users worldwide, including executives from all of the Fortune 500 companies. There were over 2 billion people searches on LinkedIn last year.

  1. Linkedin - Invite your LinkedIn Contacts to view your inventory by clicking the social media bar, which is illustrated in the graphic below on the left side. This works by showing the inventory update on your profile. This is illustrated in the graphic below on the right side. Share your inventory on LinkedIN!! You should do this enough to appear engaged but not too much to appear annoying, about 1-2 times a week is recommended.
  2. Linkedin - Join our LinkedIn Group! Meet and interact with other professionals in the Steel Pipe & Tube Business. PipeGrid - Steel Pipe & Tube Professionals
  3. Linkedin - You may also post a link to your lot in the "Share an update" box on your LinkedIn profile.
  4. Linkedin - Ask questions and participate in discussions, the group is there to answer simple questions, discuss industry news, or post inventory.
  5. Twitter - Follow us on Twitter @PipeGrid to keep up with the latest news, and inventory updates.
  6. Twitter - Connect with other Twitter users and start "Tweeting" about you, your business, whatever else you want. We will often forward or "Retweet" good posts from our followers which will expose you to our followers. Our followers can send it their followers, and so on. Sometimes this helps to establish thousands of views from just one update.
