PipeGrid Provides the Right Tools to Market Your Inventory

7 Tips for Making the Most of Your Inventory

  1. List ALL of Your Inventory - The more inventory you list the more frequently you'll appear on searches, creating additional exposure. Need help? Members posting for the first time can send their inventory list (in any format) to InventoryHelp@PipeGrid.com and have us post it to your profile for you.
  2. Keep Your Inventory Current - Nothing is more frustrating for clients than to find out that you no longer have what you've been advertising.
  3. Forward Your Inventory to Your Current Contacts on a Regular Basis - Use our "Forward Inventory" feature send your current inventory to all of your current contacts. This easy to use feature will keep you in front of your contacts. Keep in front of them by sending it on a regular basis. Once a month is recommended.
  4. Use an Ad to Drive Business to Your Site - Visit our Advertising page to see which ads would suit you best.
  5. Contribute to Our Monthly Newsletter - You can do this by updating us on new deals, awarded contracts, or with an informative industry article. You will be cited in our newsletter which is read by thousands of pipeline professionals every month. See what we're looking for on our Contribute page.
  6. Invite Your Colleagues to Join and Connect With You - You can share inventory with your connections whether they are in your organization or not. This means that multiple organizations can cooperate in inventory availability programs.
  7. Get Social - Use our powerful social media tools to help you spread the word to your current contacts and find hundreds more. Click here to visit our Social Media page.
  8. Create a Company Profile or Join your Company's Profile - Creating a company profile will increase exposure to customers and create an inbound link for your company's website. This link will help you climb Google's search results. Click here to find out more.
